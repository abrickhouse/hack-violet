How COVID-19 impacted the general wellness of everyone in the world, and changed how we look at healthcare.

In the wake of COVID-19, millions have been left with not only physical health issues, but mental issues as well. It is our goal to help the world heal from this disaster by spreading awareness and affordable, accessible care.

Problems with PPE for women

Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) like face masks, gloves, and hard hats are designed for the male body, which differs greatly from the female body. The safety statistics give by PPE manufacturers are often inaccurate, and less safe for women. Rarely is equipment, like life vests, made for pregnant bodies. Link to website
